Position: Leader

Email: eloy.ramos@iqac.csic.es

Website: https://eloyramoscordoba.wordpress.com

Experience

  • January 2024: Permanent Researcher at the Institute for Advanced Chemistry of Catalonia (IQAC-CSIC)
  • March 2023 - December 2023: Ikerbasque Fellow at the University of the Basque Country
  • July 2022 - February 2023: Researcher at Donostia International Physics Center.
  • August 2019-June 2022: Juan de la Cierva Incorporation fellow, University of the Basque Country.
  • January 2019-July 2019: Researcher at Donostia International Physics Center.
  • January 2016-December 2018: Marie Sklodowska-Curie International Outgoing fellowship (Horizon 2020). University of California, Berkeley (Prof. Head-Gordon Group). Project: AccuCT (660943)
  • January 2015- December 2015: Post-Doctoral research contract at the Donostia International Physics Center (DIPC).
  • January 2014- December 2014: Post-Doctoral research contract, funded by the Ministerio de economía y competividad (Europa excelencia project) under the lead of Dr. Eduard Matito at the Institut de Química Computacional i Catàlisi of the Universitat de Girona, Spain.

Education

  • 2014: Ph.D. in Chemistry; thesis supervised by Dr. Pedro Salvador, Universitat de Girona, Girona, Spain.
  • 2009: M.S. in Theoretical and Computational Chemistry. Universitat Rovira i Virgili, Tarragona, Spain.
  • 2008: B.S. in Chemistry, Universitat de Girona, Girona, Spain.

Research Topics

  • Electron correlation, Coulomb holes and Density Functional development.
