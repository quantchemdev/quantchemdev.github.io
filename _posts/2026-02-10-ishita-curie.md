<p style="text-align: justify; font-size: 20px;">
We are happy to share that one of our postdoctoral researches, Dr. Ishita Bhattacharjee, has obtained the prestigious Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (HORIZON) fellowship!
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify; font-size: 20px;">
The title of the accepted proposal is "Universal Multireference Diagnostics for High-Throughput Screening of Large Molecules and Solids", and it is tightly related to the Electron correlation resarch line from our group. The fellowship has a duration of two years.
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify; font-size: 20px;">
Congratulations, Ishita!
